ManaDraw – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ManaDraw (“the Application”) collects, uses, and protects user data. ManaDraw is a Discord-based Magic: The Gathering card pull simulator created for entertainment purposes only.

By using ManaDraw, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

ManaDraw collects only the minimum data necessary to operate correctly within Discord.

This may include:

Discord User IDs

Discord Server (Guild) IDs

Command interaction data (e.g., commands used, timestamps)

ManaDraw does not collect:

Email addresses

Real names

Passwords

Payment or financial information

IP addresses (beyond what Discord itself provides)

3. How We Use Your Information

Collected data is used solely to:

Respond to user commands

Enable bot features and functionality

Maintain stability and prevent abuse

Improve performance and user experience

Data is never used for advertising or tracking.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely and only for as long as necessary

Reasonable measures are taken to protect data from unauthorized access

ManaDraw does not guarantee absolute security, but best practices are followed

5. Data Sharing

ManaDraw does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

Data may only be disclosed if:

Required by law

Required to comply with Discord’s Terms of Service or legal obligations

6. Third-Party Services

ManaDraw operates entirely within Discord and relies on:

Discord’s API and infrastructure

Please review Discord’s Privacy Policy for information on how Discord handles user data.

7. Children’s Privacy

ManaDraw does not knowingly collect data from users under the minimum age required by Discord.

If you believe data from a minor has been collected unintentionally, please contact the developer for removal.

8. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Request access to your stored data

Request deletion of your data

Requests can be made through the official ManaDraw contact method (e.g., Discord support server).

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of ManaDraw after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact the ManaDraw developer via:

Discord support server (if available)

The official ManaDraw bot contact method

ManaDraw respects your privacy — shuffle, draw, and enjoy responsibly.
